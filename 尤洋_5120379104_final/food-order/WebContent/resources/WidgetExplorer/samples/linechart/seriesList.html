This sample demonstrates a set of powerful features for the Wijmo Line Chart. This is a Line Chart that shows data that compares six quarter sales of different mobile OS with interactive Legend.

Test the features

  • Hover over a data point to see the tooltip.
  • Note this chart displays several different graphs at once
  • Note the legend on the top of the chart
  • Click series items in the legend to toggle their visibility on the chart surface.
  • Play with legend options in the panel below to see its behavior.
  • Go to the 'Sources' tab to see how to setup chart legend through widget initialization
