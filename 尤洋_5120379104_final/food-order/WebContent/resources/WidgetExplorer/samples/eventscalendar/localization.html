Localization

This sample demonstrates how to localize the events calendar.

 

Options used in the sample:

  • culture - Culture name, e.g. "de-DE". Date and time formatting depends on the culture option.
  • localization - use the localization option in order to localize text which not depends on culture option.
  • dayViewHeaderFormat - format of the text for the day header in the day/week or list view.
